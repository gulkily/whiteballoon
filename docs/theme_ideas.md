Theme Ideas
===========

1. Midnight Violet — deep gradient background with neon purple accents, art deco lines, and glass morph panels.
2. Solarized Sand — sepia canvas with low-contrast text, organic textures, and cyan callouts.
3. Win95 Classic — gray chrome, pixel fonts, embossed buttons, teal title bars.
4. Terminal Green — CRT phosphor, scanline overlays, monospaced typography (already in progress).
5. Aurora Glass — multi-color aurora borealis gradient, frosted cards, aqua highlights.
6. Blueprint — navy backdrop, white drafting lines, orange dimension markers, monospace labels.
7. Neon Sunset — magenta + amber gradient, rounded cards, playful script headings.
8. Paper Notebook — cream background, dotted grid, ink-style typography, sticky-note tags.
9. Desert Mirage — warm sandstone palette, geometric shadows, turquoise buttons.
10. Arctic Minimal — icy whites, pastel blues, subtle glass blur, hairline separators.
